How about career on Web3?

🌱

It's good that you ask.
We will be creating a Web3 Education platform where students can learn and gain new skillsets.
The salary that you will be getting from Web3 is enormous (especially if you are learning Blockchain Development) 💻
Here's an image of blockchaine developer salaries from different countries (btw, most of them are remote work)...



There are also opening for non-tech jobs like Web Designers, Technical Writers, Accountants, Lawyers, Marketers & Social Media Managers, etc..
Assistance will be provided for individuals who have these skillsets. 👨‍💼👩‍💼

Part of our roadmap is to build a social network like Facebook on the blockchain...
